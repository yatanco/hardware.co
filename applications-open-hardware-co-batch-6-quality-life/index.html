Batch 6 - Quality of Life

May 2, 2017

Hardware.co is back with a new accelerator program running from July 17th to the 31st! With the world in mind this batch is themed Quality of Life, and we’re looking for startups in Hardware and IoT who have solutions to clean energy, smart cities, and connected life.

Startups in all stages are welcome, and IoT startups working with software solely have a chance too! Batch 6 can expect a highly intensive, short, two-week program packed with tailored support from industry experts. Startups get professional guidance through workshops and one-on-one sessions surrounding startup growth, product development, business, financial and legal matters, and much more. Teams have the opportunity to set up collaborations with our program partners and they’ll also gain access to Hardware.co’s larger network of leading manufacturers and distributors.

Who are the partners to this batch?

We’ve teamed up with a few giants :

Startups looking to sell their product through Germany’s leading online electronics retailer can talk to Conrad. Into clean tech and connected life? Audi is one of the worlds most specialised car manufacturers in these fields. Startups with smart solutions for mobility can talk to Deutsche Bahn . Teams into telecommunications will have a good time talking to the hardware engineers from Telefonica .

Hardware.co never takes equity nor intellectual property. Travel costs are covered for international startups, up to €200,-.

The program will run from July 17th to 31st. Applications are open from May 3rd to June 15th.
