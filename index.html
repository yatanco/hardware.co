Our Story

Born in Berlin,
built for the world.

HARDWARE.co began in 2013 as a small, informal meetup in Berlin for hardware entrepreneurs, makers, and enthusiasts to exchange advice, questions, and stories of success and failure.

The group quickly grew too large for its own meetups and pitch events. The organisers realised that interest in hardware technology flourished — both in Berlin and worldwide. To keep the community spirit while becoming professional enough to serve more people, they partnered with the founders of betahaus and established HARDWARE.co.

Today, the HARDWARE.co Accelerator, Lab, Meetups, and Online Platform form a multichannel resource giving community members the opportunity to create leading products and companies — supporting every stage of hardware development, from ideas to prototypes to investments and beyond.

The Program

Not your average accelerator.

An intensive 14-day program. Zero equity. No IP taken. No strings attached. Just world-class mentorship, real industry connections, and a Demo Day in front of Berlin's top investors and corporate partners.

01
Apply

Submit your application. Early-stage teams and individuals building hardware products of all kinds are welcome to apply.

02
Orientation

Meet your cohort, connect with mentors and corporate partners, and get aligned on your goals for the 14-day sprint.

03
Deep Dives

Intensive workshops on prototyping, DFM, business modelling, branding, legal, fundraising, and go-to-market strategy.

04
Mentors

One-on-one time with 50+ mentors across electrical engineering, mechanical design, legal, marketing, and investment.

05
Demo Day

PechaKucha-style public pitches in front of investors, VCs, and corporate partners — then join the 250+ company alumni network.
