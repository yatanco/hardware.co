Batch 6 is a wrap! Get ready for Batch 7!

August 3, 2017

Last Monday, our two-week intensive accelerator program with Batch 6 came to an end with a beautiful Show & Tell event. 8 Startups working to improve our quality of life had one minute to pitch their product. Investors, tech-experts, entrepreneurs, and other visitors wandered around in the betahaus Loft to investigate the startup stands.

They experienced the SONORRARI therapeutic sonic music vibrations; met the MIO robot delivering drinks; learned about NanoIC’s smart tag & readers system tackling the global counterfeit market – and there was so much more.

For two weeks, the startups of this batch enjoyed workshops and one-on-one sessions where they improved their skills and knowledge with regard to startup growth, product development, legal and financial matters, and value proposition marketing amongst other things. Many of the startups got their hardware tweaked and approved by German electronics experts; many made big improvements on their sales strategy, and of course – many opened conversations with partners to continue their journey together. Thanks again to Audi, Conrad, Deutsche Bahn and Telefonica for their support!

Are you interested in joining Hardware.co’s next batch?

Great. Because Batch 7 is already in the pipeline and will take place this winter. The theme of this batch will be Smart Mobility & Logistics. More information and details will follow soon, startups can already apply on F6S.

Thanks everyone for joining us! Until next time.
